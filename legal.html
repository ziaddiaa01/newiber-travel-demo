Privacy Policy - NEWIBER Travel Egypt

Information We Collect

At NEWIBER Travel, your privacy is our priority. When you book Egypt tours or Nile cruise packages through our website, we collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp details for tour consultation and booking confirmations
  • Travel Preferences: Budget, trip duration, number of travelers, and special interests (historical sites, adventure, luxury, etc.)
  • Booking Details: Selected Egypt tour packages, preferred dates, accommodation preferences, and payment information
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for website optimization

How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us provide exceptional Egypt travel experiences:

  • Process and confirm your Egypt tour bookings
  • Customize itineraries for Cairo tours, Nile cruises, and Luxor trips
  • Send booking confirmations, travel documents, and pre-departure information
  • Provide 24/7 customer support during your Egypt holiday
  • Send promotional offers for Egypt travel packages (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Improve our website and Egypt tour services

Data Protection & Security

NEWIBER Travel implements industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure payment gateways, and restricted access protocols. Your data is never sold to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may share necessary information with trusted partners including:

  • Hotels & Cruise Operators: For reservation and accommodation in Cairo, Aswan, Luxor, and during Nile cruises
  • Payment Processors: To securely handle Egypt tour bookings and transactions
  • Transportation Providers: For domestic flights, airport transfers, and ground transportation
  • WhatsApp Business: For direct communication and tour inquiries

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored by NEWIBER Travel
  • Request corrections to your booking information
  • Delete your data (subject to legal and contractual obligations)
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge complaints with relevant data protection authorities

Terms & Conditions - Egypt Tour Bookings

1. Booking & Payment Terms

  • Deposit Requirements: A non-refundable deposit of 25% is required to confirm all Egypt tour bookings. Full payment is due 30 days before departure.
  • Accepted Payment Methods: Bank transfer, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cash payments at our Cairo office
  • Pricing: All Egypt tour prices are quoted in USD. Prices include accommodation, specified meals, guided tours, entrance fees, and domestic transportation as outlined in your itinerary
  • Price Changes: Prices are subject to change until full payment is received. We reserve the right to adjust prices due to significant currency fluctuations or unforeseen cost increases

2. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • More than 60 days before departure: 25% cancellation fee (deposit forfeited)
  • 30-60 days before departure: 50% cancellation fee
  • 15-29 days before departure: 75% cancellation fee
  • Less than 15 days: 100% cancellation fee (no refund)
  • No-shows: No refund for unused services or missed tour components

3. Travel Services Provided

NEWIBER Travel provides comprehensive Egypt travel services including:

  • Egypt tour consultation and customized itinerary planning
  • Hotel and Nile cruise ship reservations (3-star to 5-star luxury)
  • Professional Egyptologist tour guides (English, Spanish, French, German available)
  • Private air-conditioned transportation and airport transfers
  • Entrance fees to Pyramids, museums, temples, and archaeological sites
  • Domestic flights between Cairo, Aswan, and Luxor (when included)
  • 24/7 emergency support throughout your Egypt holiday

4. Client Responsibilities

  • Travel Documents: Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity), Egypt tourist visa, and any required vaccinations
  • Travel Insurance: We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and personal belongings
  • Health & Safety: Inform us of any medical conditions, dietary restrictions, or mobility limitations before booking
  • Punctuality: Arrive on time for all scheduled activities, transfers, and departures
  • Conduct: Respect Egyptian customs, laws, and archaeological sites during your tour

5. Liability Limitations

While NEWIBER Travel strives for flawless Egypt tours, we are not liable for:

  • Flight delays, cancellations, or schedule changes by airlines
  • Natural disasters, political unrest, or force majeure events
  • Loss or damage to personal belongings
  • Accidents, injuries, or illness during the tour
  • Services not explicitly listed in your Egypt tour itinerary

In case of unforeseen circumstances, we will work diligently to provide suitable alternatives or reschedule your Egypt tour at no additional cost where possible.

6. Modifications & Itinerary Changes

NEWIBER Travel reserves the right to modify tour itineraries due to circumstances beyond our control, including site closures, weather conditions, or safety concerns. We will provide equivalent alternatives whenever possible.
